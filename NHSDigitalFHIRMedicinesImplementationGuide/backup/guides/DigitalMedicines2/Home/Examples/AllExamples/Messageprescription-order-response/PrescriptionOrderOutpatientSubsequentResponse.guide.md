#### Prescription Order Outpatient Subsequent Response

Response to an update message. In this example this is the subsequent repsonse to the cancellation of a outpatient prescription {{pagelink:PrescriptionOrderOutpatientCancel.md}}

This response is sent after the prescription is returned to EPS by the dispenser.
