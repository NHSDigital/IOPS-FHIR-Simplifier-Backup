## {{page-title}}

<h5><ins>Usage</ins></h5>

<span class="mro-circle mandatory" title="Mandatory"></span> Mandatory


<h5><ins>Guidance</ins></h5>

The `identifier` element **MUST** be populated with the date of birth of the patient in the format `YYYY-MM-DD`. The birth time is not required.


<h5><ins>Example</ins></h5>

```xml
<birthDate value="2009-01-04"></birthDate>
```
