<h5><ins>Example</ins></h5>

```xml
<meta>
    <security>
        <system value="http://hl7.org/fhir/v3/ActCode"/>
        <code value="NOPAT"/>
        <display value="no disclosure to patient, family or caregivers without attending provider's authorization"/>
    </security>
</meta>
```
